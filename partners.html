The model

You own the marketplace. WorkWell owns the care.

The co-brand model is simple: your name and your employer relationships on the front door. WorkWell — powered by LASO — handles everyday health access activation, support, and reporting behind it.

WorkWell is not white-labeled per partner. The LASO health access product experience stays consistent. The marketplace context is yours.

The four reusable seams

  • Branding config

    Partner name, logo, marketplace label, support contacts, co-brand language.

  • Product catalog config

    LASO package selection, approved supplemental products, pricing display, product dependencies.

  • Admin and payment config

    Merchant role, TPA path, remittance rules, effective date rules, cancellation and refund logic.

  • Reporting export config

    Enrollment, purchase, attach rate, activation, utilization, support load — formatted for your reporting needs.
